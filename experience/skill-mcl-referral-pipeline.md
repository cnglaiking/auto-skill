## Lead Management Process

### MOTOGO MCL Flow
```
Member identifies prospect
    ↓
Member submits referral via App / WhatsApp bot
    ↓
Auto-alert to BU coordinator (Dynamics 365 task + WhatsApp notification)
    ↓
BU calls prospect within 24h (SLA)
    ↓
Lead outcome logged: Interested / Booked Test Drive / Purchased / Lost
    ↓
Commission calculated and posted to MOTOGO Finance monthly
    ↓
Member receives commission notification (Push + WhatsApp)
```

### Lead Quality Standards
- Contact within 24h: mandatory SLA
- 3 contact attempts before marking "No Contact" (across 3 different days/times)
- Qualified lead = has confirmed a test drive or showroom visit
- Conversion = vehicle purchase (any DCH Motors brand)
