VIP events are Charlotte's highest-ROI single-spend items:
- **NPS booster:** Live experiences drive NPS +15–25 points vs. digital-only members

- **Media & PR:** Organic social reach 5–10× post-event amplification
- **Stakeholder management:** Board visibility, BU buy-in, partner showcase
- **Charlotte's personal brand moment:** positions her as the customer experience leader and event architect in DCH Motors

## Invitation Strategy — Personalisation at Scale

### Segmentation Logic for Invites
1. All Gold + Platinum members (automatic invite)
2. Silver members in top 30% of points earned in last 6 months
3. Members with 2+ MCL referrals (reward advocate behaviour)
4. Members who attended previous year's event (loyalty to event = loyalty to programme)
5. BU nominees: 2 per BU — staff/partner nominations from BU coordinators
