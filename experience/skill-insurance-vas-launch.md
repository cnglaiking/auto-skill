## Product Overview

### What Is MOTOGO Insurance VAS?
A value-added insurance service embedded within the MOTOGO loyalty programme, offering members preferential access to car insurance, roadside assistance, and motor protection products via partner insurers. Charlotte created this as a NEW revenue stream in 2026 — first-mover in HK automotive loyalty insurance.

## Risk Management
- **IA Compliance:** Confirm MOTOGO can earn referral fees without a broking licence (seek legal opinion Q1)
- **Partner Quality:** Vet insurer partners on claims handling — poor claims = NPS damage to MOTOGO brand
- **Data Privacy:** Insurance data requires separate consent layer; integrate with PDPO data consent framework
- **Low Uptake:** If Q2 target missed, shift to "free consultation" funnel to build pipeline before conversion push
