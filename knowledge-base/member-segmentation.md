# 👥 Member Segmentation, Personas & Targeting Rules
**Last Updated:** 2026-03-09
**Programme:** MOTOGO | FY2026

---

## 5 Core Member Personas

### 1. 🏆 VIP Champions (~5% | ~17K members)
**Profile:** High CLV, 3+ BU touchpoints, multi-vehicle, premium service users
**Behaviour:** High purchase frequency, actively refer others, redeem regularly
**Vehicle type:** Premium/luxury, multiple vehicles in household
**Annual spend w/ DCH:** HK$50,000+
**Engagement:** App + WhatsApp active, event attendees
**Marketing:** Exclusive VIP invitations, early access, personal concierge, VIP Annual Night, premium gifts
**CRM rule:** Never send generic blast. Always personalised. Dedicated relationship manager.
**Revenue contribution:** Disproportionately high — protect at all costs

### 2. ⭐ Active Loyalists (~15% | ~52K members)
**Profile:** Regular service customers, Esso fuel card users, consistent monthly touchpoints
**Behaviour:** Monthly engagement, good redemption rate, moderate referral activity
**Vehicle type:** Mid-range, 1 vehicle, service-oriented
**CRM rule:** Points booster offers, referral incentive, VAS cross-sell trigger, tier upgrade push
**Marketing:** Email + WhatsApp lifecycle, monthly newsletter, seasonal promotions
**Priority KPI:** Conversion to VIP Champions (upsell track)

### 3. 😴 Passive Members (~55% | ~190K members)
**Profile:** Registered but low engagement — <1 meaningful action per quarter
**Behaviour:** Enrolled but not buying, not redeeming, app downloaded but dormant
**Root cause:** Likely enrolled at point-of-sale without genuine interest
**CRM rule:** Re-engagement drip 3-touch: WhatsApp → Email → App push. Strong offer required.
**Marketing:** "Your rewards are waiting" messaging. Points expiry urgency. Flash offer.
**Target:** 12% reactivation rate per quarter
**Do NOT:** Spam with generic content — increases churn, not engagement

### 4. 🆕 New Members (~18% | ~62K members)
**Profile:** Enrolled in past 90 days, in onboarding phase
**Behaviour:** Exploring, low purchase yet, high potential
**CRM rule:** 3-touchpoint automated welcome journey (Day 1, Day 7, Day 14)
**Marketing:** Welcome bonus points, first purchase incentive, onboarding education
**KPI:** First Purchase Rate ≥30% within 30 days of enrolment
**Priority:** Speed to first transaction is critical — first 30 days define retention

### 5. ⚠️ At-Risk Members (~7% | ~24K members)
**Profile:** No activity in 180+ days, churning silently
**Behaviour:** Not opening emails, not visiting app, possibly migrated to competitor
**CRM rule:** Win-back campaign: strong offer (e.g. 500 bonus points + HK$50 voucher) + personal WhatsApp
**Marketing:** "We've missed you" tone. Urgency: points about to expire.
**Target:** 8% win-back conversion (from at-risk to active)
**Decision rule:** If no response in 60 days after win-back → mark inactive, remove from active campaign segments

---

## Segmentation Decision Tree

```
New member? → Welcome Journey
Last transaction < 90 days? → Active Loyalist / VIP track
Last transaction 90–180 days? → Passive re-engagement
Last transaction 180+ days? → At-Risk win-back
High CLV + multi-BU? → VIP Champions track
```

## Targeting Rules (Always Apply)

1. **Never send generic blasts** — minimum 3-segment version per campaign
2. **VIP members** — always receive first, always personalised, never promotional-feeling
3. **Passive members** — strong offer + urgency required; generic content = unsubscribe risk
4. **New members** — educational tone first, selling second; build trust before conversion
5. **At-risk members** — one strong win-back attempt, then suppress for 60 days before re-try
6. **WhatsApp** — highest engagement channel; reserve for high-value messages only (max 2/month per member)
7. **Email** — lifecycle + campaign blasts OK; target open rate ≥22%
8. **App push** — time-sensitive offers + event alerts only; respect opt-out preferences
