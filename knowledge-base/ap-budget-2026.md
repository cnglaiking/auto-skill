## Budget Governance Rules

### Approval Thresholds
- Under HK$10,000: CRM team manager can approve
- HK$10,000–HK$50,000: Charlotte approval required
- Over HK$50,000: Charlotte + GM sign-off
- Contingency spend: Charlotte approval only, with documented rationale

### Spend Rules
1. No budget reallocation between categories without Charlotte review
2. Events budget is ring-fenced — cannot be raided for digital
3. Contingency is NOT overflow — it's for genuine unplanned opportunities
4. All spend must have measurable KPI attached before approval
5. Monthly reconciliation — actual vs budget reported to Finance by 5th of each month
