## Programme Overview
MOTOGO is DCH Motors HK's flagship multi-brand loyalty programme. It connects members across 11+ business units, offering points, rewards, referral commissions, telemarketing-driven sales, and value-added services including insurance and EV charging rebates.

**Brand Promise:** *"More Than Miles — Your Complete Automotive Lifestyle Partner"*
