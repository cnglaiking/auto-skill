## BU Data Reporting Standards

### Required Data Fields (All BUs Must Submit Monthly)

| Field | Source | Format | Deadline |
|---|---|---|---|
| New member enrolments | POS / App | Member ID + date | 5th of month |
| Transaction data | POS | Member ID + amount + date | 5th of month |
| Service bookings | BU system | Member ID + service type + date | 5th of month |
| Referral submissions | MCL tracker | Referrer ID + referee ID | Weekly |
| SD Coupon redemptions | TM system | Member ID + value | Weekly |
| VAS insurance interactions | VAS partner | Member ID + status | Monthly |

### Data Quality Rules
- **Completeness:** ≥75% of member profiles fully populated by Q2 2026
- **Accuracy:** BU confirms data before submission — no corrections after 10th
- **Timeliness:** Late data = excluded from monthly report + escalated to BU manager
- **Format:** Standardised CSV template — Charlotte's team provides master template Q1
- **No duplicates:** Dynamics 365 deduplication runs monthly; BU to flag manual merges

## Cross-BU Campaign Coordination

### Campaign Briefing Protocol
1. Charlotte issues campaign brief 3 weeks before launch
2. BU CRM representatives confirm participation within 5 business days
3. Charlotte's team produces master creative assets
4. BU adapts for local audience (if needed) — within brand guidelines
5. All adaptations approved by Charlotte before deployment
6. Results consolidated at Charlotte's team level within 2 weeks post-campaign

### BU-Specific Customisation Rules
- BU can customise product offers within the campaign mechanic
- BU cannot change campaign mechanic, brand identity, or KPI measurement approach
- Charlotte's team owns all member communication sign-off
