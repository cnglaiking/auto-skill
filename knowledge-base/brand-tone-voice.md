## Tone of Voice

### Primary Tone: CONFIDENT & WARM
- Sound like a knowledgeable friend who knows cars, not a corporate chatbot
- Never stiff, never jargon-heavy, never generic
- Always relevant to the specific member — use their name, vehicle, history

### Secondary Tone: CLEAR & DIRECT
- Members are busy. Get to the point.
- One CTA per communication. One clear benefit per message.
- Never bury the value. Lead with the benefit.

### Tertiary Tone: FORWARD-THINKING
- Reflect DCH Motors' innovation story (EV, AI, digital transformation)
- Light optimism — "what's next" energy, never retrospective
- Subtly sophisticated — premium without being cold

## Language Rules

### English
- Use contractions: "you've", "we're", "it's" — feels human
- Active voice always: "Earn double points" NOT "Double points can be earned"
- Short sentences under campaigns: max 20 words for headlines
- Benefit-first: "Save HK$500 on your next service" NOT "Our service discount programme offers..."
- Avoid: corporate buzzwords ("leverage", "synergy", "ecosystem" in customer comms)

### Traditional Chinese (Cantonese-flavoured)
- Use 您 for VIP/formal comms; 你 for regular member comms
- Cantonese idiom awareness: copy should feel natural to HK Chinese speaker, not translated Mandarin
- Keep promotional copy punchy — 4-character phrases work well for headlines
- Always bilingual for major campaigns: EN headline + TC body or vice versa

## Campaign Headline Formulas

**For acquisition:**
- "Join MOTOGO. Drive More. Save More."
- "Your car. Your rewards. Your MOTOGO."

**For activation/engagement:**
- "Double points this [month]. Don't miss out."
- "You've earned [X] points. Time to use them."

**For VAS / Insurance:**
- "Protect Your Drive. Exclusive rates for MOTOGO members."

**For events:**
- "[Event Name] — By invitation only for MOTOGO VIP members."

**For re-engagement:**
- "[Name], your [X] points expire in 30 days."
- "Still driving? Your rewards are waiting."

## What NOT To Say (Brand Voice Violations)

| ❌ Avoid | ✅ Use Instead |
|---|---|
| "Dear Valued Customer" | "[Name]" always |
| "Please be advised that..." | Direct statement |
| "Leverage our ecosystem" | "Access your rewards" |
| "We are pleased to inform you" | Just say it |
| "Terms and conditions apply" as opener | T&C in footer only |
| Generic "congratulations on joining" | Specific welcome with benefit |
| Exclamation marks in every sentence | Use sparingly — only for genuine excitement |

## Visual Brand Guidelines (Summary)

**Primary Colours:**
- DCH Motors Navy: #002060 (authority, trust, premium)
- MOTOGO Gold: #C9A84C (reward, prestige, energy)
- Clean White: #FFFFFF (clarity, space)
- Mid Grey: #F5F5F5 (backgrounds, separators)

**Typography Feel:** Clean, modern sans-serif. Headlines bold, body text readable. No decorative fonts for corporate comms.

**Imagery Style:** Real HK environments, real driving scenarios, aspirational but authentic. Avoid stock photo clichés. Feature actual DCH vehicle lineup where possible.

**Logo Usage:** MOTOGO logo always on dark navy or white background. Never on busy photo backgrounds.

---

## Bilingual Content Rules

- All major campaign assets: bilingual (EN + TC)
- Digital ads: A/B test EN vs TC for targeting optimisation
- WhatsApp: Default to member's preferred language (captured at sign-up)
- Events: Bilingual signage, bilingual host script
- App: Full TC localisation required (not just translation — localisation)
